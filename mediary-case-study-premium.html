Learnings & Reflections

Key takeaways from this project

‚úÖ What Worked Well

  • Early and frequent doctor involvement in testing
  • Mobile-first approach for clinical workflows
  • Investing in robust design system upfront
  • AI tools accelerated iteration cycles
  • HIPAA compliance integrated from day one

üîÑ What I'd Do Differently

  • Test with specialists earlier (not just PCPs)

  • Include more audio quality edge cases in testing

üéì Key Learnings

  • Trust is earned through accuracy, not speed
  • Doctors value reliability over fancy features

  • Simple workflows win in clinical settings
