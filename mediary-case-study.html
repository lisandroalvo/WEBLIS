Project Overview

The Problem

Doctors spend 2-3 hours daily on clinical documentation, often working after hours. This administrative burden leads to:

  • Physician burnout and decreased job satisfaction
  • Reduced face-to-face time with patients
  • Increased risk of documentation errors
  • Lower quality of patient care
  • Delayed insurance claim processing

The Solution

MEDIARY is an AI-powered ambient medical scribe that revolutionizes clinical documentation:

  • Listens to doctor-patient conversations in real-time
  • Automatically generates structured SOAP notes
  • Provides accurate E&M (Evaluation and Management) coding
  • Creates instant visit summaries
  • Integrates seamlessly via Supabase backend
