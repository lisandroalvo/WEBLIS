The Challenge

Doctors spend 2-3 hours daily on clinical documentation, often working after hours. Current AI medical note-takers are complicated, don't integrate with existing workflows, and require extensive training to use effectively.

  • High burnout rates: Doctors cite documentation as #1 stressor
  • Reduced patient time: 45% of doctor's time spent on paperwork
  • Error-prone: Manual entry leads to coding mistakes
  • After-hours work: 2+ hours nightly on clinical notes
üí°

Pain Points Identified

  • Existing solutions are clunky and disruptive
  • High cost with low ROI for small practices
  • Limited integration with EHR systems
  • Privacy concerns with cloud-based AI
  • Steep learning curve for adoption

The Solution

üí°

MEDIARY: Intelligent Ambient AI Medical Scribe

MEDIARY is an intelligent ambient AI medical scribe that listens silently to doctor-patient conversations, automatically generates structured SOAP notes, provides accurate E&M coding for billing, creates real-time visit summaries, integrates seamlessly with Supabase backend, and maintains full HIPAA compliance‚Äîrequiring zero training to use.

The result: Doctors save 1-2 hours daily and can focus entirely on patient care instead of documentation.
